# Avoiding Whisper Stocks

In the world of investing, there is a very dangerous creature called the **Whisper Stock**. It is a stock that someone tells you about in a quiet voice, as if it were a holy secret. It is a fairy tale for adults who don't want to perform the [Two-Minute Drill](/guide/the-two-minute-drill/).

It is usually a small company with a big dream: a "cure for cancer," a "car that runs on water," or a "machine that turns base metal into gold." It has no earnings. It has no products. It only has a "whisper." If you find yourself in the middle of a whisper, you should start thinking about [When to Sell](/guide/when-to-sell/).

Peter Lynch, a man who liked to see the money before he gave it his own, realized that if you buy a whisper stock, you are an artist in search of a tragedy.


#### The Miracles
*   **The Miracle**: The company is going to change everything. They are "disrupting" a massive industry before they've even sold a single widget.
*   **The FOMO**: You are afraid you will miss out on the next Apple. This is a very efficient way to buy a lemon.
*   **The Lack of Proof**: If it's a miracle, it should be in the earnings. If the earnings are zero, it is not a miracle. It is a fairy tale.
