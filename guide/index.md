# The Hitchhiker's Guide to Peter Lynch Investing


### Welcome to the guide. 
It is a guide to the stock market, which is a large, invisible machine designed to move money from the nervous to the patient. It is also an remarkably improbable place where common sense is often treated as a forbidden technology.

Peter Lynch was a man who understood the machine. He was not a machine himself, which is why he was so good at it. He used things called "eyes" and "ears" to find companies that were actually doing something useful, rather than just shuffling digital bits in a high-frequency server rack.

We have built this guide because human beings are prone to forgetting. We forget that a stock is a piece of a business, not a number on a flickering terminal. We have categorized these lessons into ten volumes. You should read them as if your survival in the financial universe depended on it.

Here are the ten lessons you need to master. Don't Panic.
